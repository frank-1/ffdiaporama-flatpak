Render movies

Introduction

Contrary to music files which are widely portable from a device to the other one, for videos, it is necessary to juggle between the various formats to adapt itself to the equipment which will be used to read them.

Indeed, a video planned for a "Home Cinema" device can weigh some Go and will be unreadable on Smartphone.
The same video planned for Smartphone will require only some Mb, can be read on a "Home Cinema" device but will be ugly.

It's why we recommend you to preserve as much as possible your projects files and to generate movies according to the device.

To simplify the settings of the render movie operation, ffDiaporama contains a device for the following device:
  • Smartphone and portable device and in a general way small devices allowing to read a video.
  • Multimedia system for all lounge and "Home Cinema" devices
  • For WEB (Blogs, sharing and social sites)
It is also possible to define each parameters through an advanced option.

Smartphones and tablets

Generally speaking the main differences between these equipments are:
  • Screen size and maximal resolution
  • Operating system on the device
Consult the specification sheet of your equipment or make a look for on internet to know these two characteristics.
For the resolution, note that equipments can generally read videos of lower sizes without difficulties.

Multimedia system (Home cinema)

This "Home Cinema" devices are connected to a television. The format choice will thus be made according to the capacity of the television.

Warning: The old SD format is only adapted to the old television using the cathode ray tubes technology. With these tubes, points were not round but ovals: we speak about anamorphism.

To read a video using SD format, on a modern television set in LCD, LED or Plasma gives openly disastrous results: The image is particularly ugly! Thus, if your television is recent and if your device allows it, always prefer a HD or Full-HD format to a SD format.

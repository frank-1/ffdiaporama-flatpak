Translation

FfDiaporama is developed in English by a French speaker - It is possible that there are grammatical mistakes in English: if you see it do not hesitate to let it to us know.
FfDiaporama is translated into French by his author - the Same remark, if you see faults do not hesitate!

For other languages: everything is to be made

If you wish to contribute to the project by translating ffDiaporama in your tongue, you can download one of the following files, complete it by using Qt Linguist and send it back to us by email at the following address: levray.dominique _@_ bbox.fr or by the forum (If the language in which you wish to translate ffDiaporama is not listed here, send us an email.)

Tutorials and articles

A documentation will never replace experience feedback.
If you create tutorials or draft articles on ffDiaporama, send us link towards your blog and we shall add it to this site.

Graphical elements

FfDiaporama contains a library of graphic elements: LUMA images, background images, icons, etc....
If you conceive compatible graphic elements with ffDiaporama and want to propose it to us, we shall to add them to list of what we supply with the program.

Devices database

One of our wishes is to maintain the device database profils to render video most up to date as possible.
If you know the ideal parameters for device, do not hesitate to communicate them to us.

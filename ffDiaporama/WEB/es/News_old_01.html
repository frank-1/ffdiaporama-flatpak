Switch to BETA

After a while, it is necessary to decide to take out a stable version!
For a long time, the strategy was to say that as long as the TODO-LIST would not be emptied, we would continue with alpha, but it is necessary to see the evidence: the more we add feature and the more we have new ideas and news entered in the TODO-LIST. Result: instead of emptying, it fills !
With this rhythm there, there will be never stable version. Thus, to mark a point of stage, we switch to beta!

Concretely, it means:
  • There will be no more new feature up to the stable version
  • The bugg hunting is opened more than ever!
According to the declarations of buggs, there will be (or not) a beta 2. The objective is if possible, to take out the (stable) version 1.0 before the end of August.

06/05/2011 - ffDiaporama 0.99.alpha2
The alpha2 release justifies itself by the modification of the universe of slides:
  • The Alpha1 version (as videoporama) defined slides as being an image or a video to which applied a composition global
  • Every slide consisted of a series of couple of fixed/mobil shot.
  • Every couple of plan possessed a composition
In the new universe:
  • Every slide is defined as being a composition and a series of shot
  • For every plan of the series we define the properties of the blocks of the composition (blocks exist in all the shots but can be masked shot by shot)
  • The blocks of composition can be of the text, a photo or a video
These major modifications implies a deep modification of the interface and the file of data.
( Note: sorry but the upward compatibility of the ancient savings is not done

Earnings for the interface:
  • The interface of edition does not consist more than of the only dialogue box of composition.
  • This box call up by buttons of edition of text, with framming/correction, with filter and video edition
  • It becomes much simpler to use
Earnings features :
  • It becomes possible to have several images / video on the same slide
  • It becomes possible to reduce the display of the images / videos: they do not occupy any more necessarily all the screen
  • It becomes possible to add animations of location of the images / videos on the screen (besides the effects burns ken)
  • It becomes possible to play with the back location forward/backward between blocks
  • It becomes possible to apply a shape to the images / videos (circle, triangle, etc.) while before, only the rectangle was possible
In the days which come, the Web site and the WIKI will be completely redone and pages of tutorial presenting these new features will make their appearance.

04/25/2011 - Availability of the first test version : ffDiaporama 0.99.alpha1
Having participated in the development (in Python) of 2 versions of Videoporama (http://videoporama.tuxfamily.org), i decided to begin again a new application (in QT).
As Videoporama, it is a of creation of videos sequences established by :
  • titles, fixed or livened up.
  • images or by photos, fixed or livened up.
  • movie clip
  • music
These sequences are assembled in slide show by means of transitions of sequence to produce complete videos
For ffDiaporama, i resumed most of the features of Videoporama:
  • Refocused of images and photos
  • Cutting of video clips
  • Note (addition of text) for images, photos, sequences and animations
  • Creation of animation by zoom or Ken Burns Effect on part of images or photos
  • Transitions between sequence with definition of the transition type, sequence by sequence.
  • Addition of a background sound (wav, mp3 or ogg)
  • Generation of usable videos by most of the current videos equipments (DVD player / smartphone, multimedia box, hard drive, etc.) but also publishable on the main video sharing Web sites (YouTube, Dailymotion, etc.)
  • Video format from VGA (640x4800) to Full HD (1920x1080) by way of the DVD and HD 720 formats.
  • Image geometry (aspect ratio) : 4:3 or 16:9
  • Possible formats of rendering : avi, mpg, mp4, mkv
And, I took advantage of it to add it quite a lot of features which did not still exist, as:
  • Real time preview
  • Graphic filters on the images and the videos (passage in black and white, dust removal, equalization of colors, etc.)
  • Rotation in the animations
  • Customizable effects of volume, fade in/out and passage in pause, sequence by sequence.
  • Image geometry (aspect ratio) 2.35:1 (cinema)
Indeed on, I am always in touch with the Videoporama team, and discussions are in progress so that my application becomes future version major of Videoporama.
In the meantime, that is until ffDiaporama become stable, both applications are going to continue to live each.
Today I put on-line the first test version. It stays some more of details to be adjusted but the main part of the work is made.
